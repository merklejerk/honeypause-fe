Welcome to HoneyPause.net!

HoneyPause is a permissionless on-chain exploit bounty tied to a circuit breaker. Whitehats can and atomically prove a smart contract exploit on-chain, pause the affected protocol, then collect a bounty for it. Protocols opt into the system by registering a bounty on the smart contract. The entire system is permissionless, non-custodial, and free! See the github for more info on how to participate.
